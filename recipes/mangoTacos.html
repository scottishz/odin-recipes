Mango Tacos

Did you forget to finish your chores during the day and need to make a meal to apologize for it. These declicious mango tacos will make them forget all about it right away. They will be so consumed with the meal and you will be home free.

Ingredients

  • 2 Mangos
  • Baja fish
  • Tortilla
  • 1 can of cooked black beans
  • 1 cup of cabbage
  • Siracha Mayo

Recipe

  1. Prepare Fish: Pat the fish dry with paper towels and season with 1/4 tsp. salt. Place fish in a small baking dish (8x8" or 9x9") or wide-rimmed bowl. In a small bowl, combine 2 Tbsp. olive oil, orange juice, and taco seasoning; whisk to combine. Pour mixture over fish and gently turn fish to coat in marinade. Cover and refrigerate for a minimum of 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours.
  2. Prepare Mango Salsa: In a large bowl, combine mango, red bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, jalape√±o, lime juice, cumin, and salt; mix to combine. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  3. Prepare Baja Sauce and Slaw: In a small bowl, combine Greek yogurt (or sour cream), mayonnaise, lime juice, Old Bay Seasoning, and a dash of hot sauce (if using); whisk to combine.
  4. In a separate large bowl, add shredded cabbage (or coleslaw) and season with a pinch of salt. Add 3 Tbsp. of the Baja Sauce and reserve remaining for topping. Toss slaw to combine and set aside.
  5. Cook the Fish: Heat remaining 2 Tbsp. olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Once hot, add the marinated fish and cook, undisturbed, for about 4 minutes. Gently flip, and cook for another 3 to 4 minutes, until the fish is firm to the touch. Transfer fish to a plate and flake into large chunks
  6. Assemble Tacos: Place a small amount of slaw in each tortilla, followed by the fish, mango salsa, and a finishing drizzle of remaining Baja Sauce.
