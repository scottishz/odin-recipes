Honey-Lime Chicken

Parents in law coming over this weekend? Need something to impress them? This dish will wow their boots off.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 tbsp. honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp. McCormick Ground Tumeric
  • 1/4 tsp. McCormick Ground Ginger
  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • Kosher salt
  • McCormick Pure Ground Black Pepper
  • 2 c. white rice, cooked, for serving

Recipe

  1. Make marinade: Whisk together 2 tablespoons olive oil, lime juice, honey, garlic, McCormick Ground Tumeric, and McCormick Ground Ginger.
  2. Add chicken and toss to evenly coat with the marinade. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour and up to 2 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 425Â°. In a large, oven-proof skillet over medium-high heat, heat enough olive oil to coat the bottom of the pan. Season both sides of marinated chicken with salt
  4. Add chicken to pan skin-side down. Sear until skin becomes golden and crispy, about 6 minutes. Flip chicken thighs, and cook 2 minutes more. Turn off the heat, pour in remaining marinade, and transfer pan into hot oven.
  5. Bake until chicken is cooked through, about 10 to 12 minutes more.
  6. Serve with white rice and drizzle with extra pan drippings.
